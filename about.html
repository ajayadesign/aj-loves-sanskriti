Our Story

AJ loves Sanskriti was born from a deep love for family and the precious moments shared with our little ones. Inspired by my own journey into parenthood, I wanted to create a brand that nurtures these special times and supports families in making lasting memories.

Every product and service is crafted with care, keeping your family's well-being at heart. It's our mission to ensure you feel supported, cherished, and celebrated every step of the way.

Our Mission

At AJ loves Sanskriti, we believe in celebrating family values and nurturing every precious moment of parenthood. Our mission is to provide families with thoughtful, high-quality baby care solutions that bring warmth, comfort, and joy to your home.

Our Core Values

Family First

Everything we do is rooted in love and care for families, ensuring you feel supported every step of the way.

Sustainability

We are committed to offering eco-friendly products that are safe for your baby and kind to the planet.

Quality & Care

From the materials we choose to the services we provide, quality and care are at the heart of our process.
